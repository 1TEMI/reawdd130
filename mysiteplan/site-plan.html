Content

Home page

The home page will serve as the entry point to the website and will feature a combination of content elements, including a welcome message, an overview of our company, highlights of featured products or services, and calls to action to encourage visitors to explore further. The page will also have ABOUT US section which provides background information about the company, including its history, mission, values, and key personnel. It may also highlight achievements and or awards that help establish credibility and trust.

People can see the website name and a banner at the top of this page. As they scroll down, they will find a grid with 6 tips and tricks in the form of cards, detailing how to fix minor issues with their PCs and speed up their computers. How they can place an order to an items or services.

Some of the questions they might have include:

* I don't have a lot of files, so why does my computer have no space?
* How do I clean out my computer's temporary files and trash?
* how do I optimize Windows for my outdated computer?
* how do I place an order for item or services?
* How can I check the specs on my computer?
* What is our return policy on purchased items?
